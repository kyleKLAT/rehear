In this Privacy Policy, "Parati," "we," "us," and "our" refer to G.M.I. Technology Inc.
"G.M.I. Affiliates" means G.M.I. Corporation, the ultimate parent company of G.M.I., and any legal entities controlled by G.M.I. Corporation.
"G.M.I. Group" refers collectively to G.M.I. Technology Inc., G.M.I. Corporation, and their controlled affiliates.
The term "control" means the direct or indirect ownership of at least fifty percent (50%) of the voting rights in an entity or the ability to direct its management decisions.

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable international data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Taiwan Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

- We use the Collected Data (defined below) to provide and improve the features and functions of the Parati Application, and to deliver related content and services.
- You have certain data protection rights, including the right to object to some types of processing and to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

2. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Parati may collect the following information (collectively, the "Collected Data") automatically when you use the Application:

(i) Information about your device on which the Application is installed ("Installed Device")
- Device ID automatically assigned, specifications, and features (e.g., product type, manufacturer, model name)
- Operating system and version
- Language, country/region, time zone
- Operational status of the Installed Device and its components (e.g., sensors, display)
- IP address used by the Installed Device

(ii) Information about the Application
- Application ID automatically assigned (unique identifier)
- Application version
- Time stamps related to your use of the Application
- Usage status and history of the Application

(iii) Information about connected Parati devices ("Related Device")
- Device ID (e.g., Bluetooth address, serial number, registration ID) and specifications/features (product type, device name, manufacturer, model name)
- Operational status and settings of the Related Device (e.g., software version, language, region)
- Usage status and history (e.g., functions in use, connected devices, error logs)
- Technical information about audio playback (e.g., codec used)

The Collected Data is uploaded with a unique identifier assigned to the Installed Device, allowing the G.M.I. Group to distinguish your device's data from other devices.

(a) Providing and improving services
We use the Collected Data to:

If you select "Agree and Proceed" when prompted, we will process your data based on your consent.

We may also process your data where necessary to pursue our legitimate interests, including:
- Identifying and preventing security threats, unauthorized access, or data breaches

- Enforcing our Terms of Use or other policies

The Collected Data may be shared as follows:
